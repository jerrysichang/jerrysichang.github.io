Fund Online Application

Prospective investors needed a streamlined online application process. One smart enough to cater for multiple investor types with varying conditional requirements, fully responsive and streamlined to reduce load on support services.

As lead UI/UX designer I:

  • Facilitated requirement gathering workshops with the client and subject matter experts.
  • Ran user-flow co-design sessions.
  • Weekly, iterative wireframe design sharebacks with varying business stakeholders.
  • Fully responsive form design.
  • UI design with developer handoff.

For a financial services group: Moelis Australia

Financial services group Moelis Australia needed a new online investment fund application form. The goal of the project was to create an industry-leading customer experience, that would lead to lower customer enquiries and an increased conversion rate. Existing customerâ€™s pain points were provided and supplemented with data from customer interviews and internal user tests of the existing form. Other investment fund applications (both paper and online) were analysed for effective vernacular and familiar design patterns, and the project involved working closely with multiple subject matter experts and stakeholders to understand the complex requirements for a successful investment fund application.

After testing and revising several iterations, the final form design was built using modular sections that could be modified or enhanced with messaging and tooltips over time through ongoing user-testing and feedback post-launch. The visual design of the online application is built around consistent spacing blocks and a robust typographic scale, in order to accomodate the level of structure and clarity needed for a flexible, yet information-dense design layout.
